## Overview

TBC Wallet simplifies mining management, helping both new and seasoned users maximize returns securely. Our integrated mining feature allows you to connect to mining pools and track your rewards directly within the wallet application.

## How It Works

### Connecting to Mining Pools

TBC Wallet supports connections to major mining pools across multiple blockchain networks. You can easily connect your wallet to a mining pool of your choice and start earning rewards.

1. **Select a Mining Pool**: Choose from our list of verified mining pools
2. **Connect Your Wallet**: Securely connect your TBC Wallet to the selected pool
3. **Start Mining**: Begin earning rewards automatically
4. **Track Performance**: Monitor your mining statistics in real-time

### Real-Time Tracking

Track your mining performance directly in the app:

- **Hash Rate**: Monitor your contribution to the mining pool
- **Rewards**: View accumulated and pending rewards
- **Earnings History**: Access detailed transaction history
- **Performance Metrics**: Analyze your mining efficiency

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites

Before you begin mining with TBC Wallet, ensure you have:

- A TBC Wallet account with sufficient balance
- Stable internet connection
- Understanding of basic mining concepts

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Open TBC Wallet**: Launch the application on your device
2. **Navigate to Mining**: Go to the Features section and select "Miner"
3. **Choose a Pool**: Browse and select a mining pool that suits your needs
4. **Configure Settings**: Set your mining preferences and parameters
5. **Start Mining**: Activate mining and begin earning rewards

### Best Practices

- **Regular Monitoring**: Check your mining status regularly
- **Pool Selection**: Research pools before connecting
- **Security**: Never share your private keys or credentials
- **Updates**: Keep your wallet updated to the latest version

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is mining safe with TBC Wallet?

Yes, mining through TBC Wallet is secure. All connections are encrypted, and your private keys remain on your device at all times.

### How often are rewards distributed?

Reward distribution depends on the mining pool you're connected to. Most pools distribute rewards daily or weekly.

### Can I mine multiple cryptocurrencies simultaneously?

Yes, TBC Wallet supports mining multiple cryptocurrencies, but you'll need to connect to different pools for each network.

### What are the minimum requirements?

Minimum requirements vary by network and pool. Generally, you'll need sufficient balance to cover transaction fees and pool requirements.

### How do I withdraw my mining rewards?

Rewards are automatically added to your wallet balance. You can withdraw them like any other asset in your wallet.
