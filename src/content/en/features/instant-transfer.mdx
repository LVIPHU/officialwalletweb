## Overview

TBC Wallet's Instant Transfer feature enables lightning-fast cross-chain transactions with minimal fees and near-instant settlement. Whether you're sending funds to friends, funding dApps, or moving assets between networks, Instant Transfer makes it seamless and efficient.

## How It Works

### Cross-Chain Technology

Instant Transfer leverages advanced cross-chain protocols to enable asset transfers between different blockchain networks without the traditional delays and high costs associated with bridge transactions.

1. **Select Assets**: Choose the asset and amount you want to transfer
2. **Choose Destination**: Select the target blockchain network
3. **Confirm Transaction**: Review and confirm the transfer details
4. **Instant Settlement**: Assets arrive at the destination almost immediately

### Supported Transfer Types

- **Peer-to-Peer**: Send directly to other wallet addresses
- **Cross-Chain**: Transfer between different blockchain networks
- **dApp Funding**: Quickly fund decentralized applications
- **Exchange Deposits**: Fast deposits to exchange platforms

## How to Use

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Open TBC Wallet**: Launch the application
2. **Navigate to Transfer**: Go to the Transfer section
3. **Select Instant Transfer**: Choose the Instant Transfer option
4. **Enter Details**: Input recipient address and amount
5. **Choose Network**: Select source and destination networks
6. **Review & Confirm**: Check all details and confirm
7. **Complete**: Transaction processes instantly

### Best Practices

- **Verify Addresses**: Always double-check recipient addresses

## Fees

### Fee Structure

Instant Transfer fees are calculated based on:

- **Network Costs**: Base fees for each blockchain network
- **Transfer Amount**: Some networks charge based on transaction size
- **Priority**: Optional priority fees for faster processing

### Fee Comparison

Compared to traditional bridge transfers:

- **Lower Costs**: Typically 50-70% lower than bridge fees
- **No Hidden Fees**: Transparent pricing upfront
- **Batch Discounts**: Lower fees for multiple transfers

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How fast are Instant Transfers?

Most transfers complete within 10-30 seconds, depending on network congestion and the specific chains involved.

### Are Instant Transfers safe?

Yes, Instant Transfer uses verified cross-chain protocols and includes multiple security layers to ensure safe transactions.

### What are the minimum transfer amounts?

Minimum amounts vary by network and asset type. Check the transfer interface for specific limits.

### Can I cancel an Instant Transfer?

Once confirmed, Instant Transfers cannot be cancelled as they process almost immediately. Always verify details before confirming.

### Are there daily transfer limits?

Yes, daily limits apply for security reasons. Limits vary based on your account verification level.

### Which networks are supported?

TBC Wallet supports Instant Transfer between TBChain, Bitcoin, Ethereum, Tron, and other major networks. The list is continuously expanding.
