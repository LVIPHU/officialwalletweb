## Support Topics

### What We Can Help With

Our support team can assist with:

- **Account Issues**: Wallet setup, recovery, and management

To help us assist you better, please have ready:

- **Wallet Version**: Your current TBC Wallet version
- **Device Information**: Device type and operating system

- **Transaction Details**: Transaction hash if related to a transaction

## Security Notice

### Important Security Information

- **Never Share**: Never share your recovery phrase, private keys, or passwords
- **Official Channels Only**: Only use official TBC Wallet channels for support
- **Verify Sources**: Always verify you're contacting the official TBC Wallet team
- **Report Scams**: Report any suspicious activity immediately

### What We Will Never Ask For

TBC Wallet support will **never** ask for:

- Your recovery phrase or seed words
- Your private keys
- Your wallet password
- Payment for support services

If someone claiming to be from TBC Wallet asks for this information, it's a scam. Report it immediately.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent security issues, we prioritize and respond as quickly as possible.

### Can I get phone support?

Currently, we provide support through email and our community channels. Phone support may be available for enterprise customers.

### Is support available in multiple languages?

Yes, our support team can assist in multiple languages. Please specify your preferred language when contacting us.

### How do I report a bug?

To report a bug:

1. Email us at contact@tbchatofficial.com
2. Include detailed description of the issue
3. Provide steps to reproduce the bug
4. Include screenshots if possible
5. Specify your device and app version
